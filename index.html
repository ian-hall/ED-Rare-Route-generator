Elite: Dangerous Rare Routes

A program I put together to generate rare trade routes for the game Elite: Dangerous. Uses a genetic algorithm to throw a bunch of systems together until it gets a group of systems that isn't completely horrible.

If you don't know what any of this means, you can go to my other page to make some fake rare items: Elite Rares. Also available on Twitter!
